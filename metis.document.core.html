ids

(ids {mp-id :mp-id, :as m})

Returns the vector of ids added to the short term memory.

Example:

(add {:mp-id "test"} "aaa")
;; ...
(ids "test")
;; [aaa]

store-results

(store-results m results doc-path)(store-results conf {mp-id :mp-id, :as m} results doc-path)

Stores the results vector under the doc-path of every document active at the given mp-id.

Example:

(def results [{:Type "cmp-test" :Unit "Pa" :Value 1}
             {:Type "cmp-test2" :Unit "Pa" :Value 2}])

(def doc-path  "Calibration.Measurement.Values.Pressure")  

(store-results {:mp-id "ref"} results doc-path)
