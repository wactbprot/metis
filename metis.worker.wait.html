metis.worker.wait

wait!

(wait! {wait-time :WaitTime, :as task} m)

Delays the mp for the time given with :WaitTime.

Example:

(wait! {:WaitTime 1000 :mp-id "test" :struct :cont :func :defin :no-idx 0 :par-idx 0 :seq-idx 0})
