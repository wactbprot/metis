map->safe-map

(map->safe-map m)(map->safe-map {a :at-replace} m)

Replaces all of the @-signs (if followed by letters 1) by a %-sign because :%kw is a valid keyword but :@kw not (or at least problematic).

1) There are devices annotating channels by (@101:105). This expressions should remain as they are.
