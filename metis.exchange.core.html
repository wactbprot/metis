enclose-map

(enclose-map m p)

Encloses the given map m with respect to the key k.

Example:

(enclose-map {:gg "ff"} "mm.ll")
;; gives:
;; {"mm" {:ll {:gg "ff"}}}

(enclose-map {:gg "ff"} "mm")
;; gives:
;; {"mm" {:gg "ff"}}

(enclose-map {"mm" "ff"} nil)
;; gives:
;; {"mm" "ff"}
